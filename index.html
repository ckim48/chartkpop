About K-House

K-House is an innovative platform dedicated to exploring and sharing the dynamic and ever-evolving world of Korean pop culture. Whether you are a long-time enthusiast or a newcomer to the K-pop scene, our platform is designed to provide an accessible and comprehensive resource for staying informed on the latest releases. We offer a curated catalog of various official K-pop merchandise which can be shipped internationally.

01 Data Analytics

Data science plays a crucial role in analyzing large volumes of user data to uncover patterns and trends. In this context, it helps track user behaviors and preferences across the online store, providing valuable insights to improve customer experience and drive business decisions.

02 Facial Expression Analysis

Machine learning models, including advanced facial analysis, can detect emotions as users interact with products. By analyzing expressions and reactions, these models help us understand how users feel about certain items. This allows us to personalize recommendations, creating a more engaging and tailored experience just for customers.

03 Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis uses natural language processing (NLP) to analyze reviews and feedback from customers. By determining the emotional tone behind the feedback, businesses can identify how customers feel about specific products and make data-driven improvements.
